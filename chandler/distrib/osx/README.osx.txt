Chandler README.osx
-------------------

* Compatibility *

Chandler runs under Mac OS X 10.4 or greater on both the PowerPC and Intel Macs

* Running Chandler *

To run chandler, first open the .dmg image and drag the Chandler application
icon to your desktop or somewhere else on your filesystem.

Next, double-click the Chandler application icon.  (If you try to double-click
on the Chandler icon without copying it from the .dmg disk image, Chandler
will give you a cryptic error.)
